2. How We Use Your Information

  • Customer Support – to respond to questions or comments you send us.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share it only:

4. Cookies & Similar Technologies

The site uses only essential cookies needed for core functionality and security. We do not use cookies for advertising or analytics that track you across sites. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function properly.

We retain your name and email only as long as necessary to answer your inquiry and for a short period afterward to maintain records, unless a longer period is required by law.

We employ reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the limited information we hold. However, no online transmission or storage system is guaranteed to be 100 % secure.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please use our Contact Us form and indicate your request. We will verify your identity and respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have done so, we will delete it promptly.

Our website may link to third‑party sites we do not control. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those sites. Review their privacy statements before providing information.
