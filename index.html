Rooted in Community

Founded Feb 4 2025 in a restored cottage at 22735 Antique Ln, Shouting Grounds Coffee Co. was created by locals Katie & Douglas Carroll as a Psalm 100-inspired space where neighbors can “shout for joy” over great coffee and conversation.
