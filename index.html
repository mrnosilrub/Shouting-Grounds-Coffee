Rooted in Community

Shouting Grounds opened its doors on February 4, 2025, inside a lovingly restored home at 22735 Antique Lane. Local founders Katie & Douglas Carroll chose the name after Psalm 100, which invites believers to “shout for joy to the Lord.” Their vision is simple: share Christ’s love one cup at a time and give New Caney a gathering place where neighbors feel seen, heard, and caffeinated.

Inside you’ll find comfy nooks, free Wi-Fi, and worship music humming softly in the background—perfect for morning devotionals, business huddles, or a slow Saturday chat. Every bean is roasted to pull its loudest notes; every pastry is mixed, proofed, and baked before sunrise to greet the first customers of the day.
