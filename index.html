Rooted in Community

Owned and operated by New Caney locals Katie & Douglas Carroll, Shouting Grounds opened its doors in 2025 with a simple mission: share Christ’s love one cup at a time. From signature espresso drinks to smoothies and scratch-baked pastries, every item is crafted with purpose and served with a smile.

Our cozy space is designed for connection—grab a seat, strike up a conversation, or sink into a good book while the aroma of freshly ground beans fills the air.
